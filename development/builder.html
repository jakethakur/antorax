Damage: "x" Weapons only. Changes damage dealt from an attack. Can interact with some spells and abilities to decide how strong they are. 0
Defense: "x" Reduces damage taken (quadratically). 0
Critical_Chance: "+x%" Changes chance of dealing double damage with an attack. 1%
Dodge_Chance: "+x%" Changes chance of ignoring an enemy attack. 1% Don't give any individual item more than 10-15% without a suitable downside, otherwise a player could be able get near 100%!
Flaming: "+x" Weapons only. Makes your attacks flaming, meaning 1 damage is dealt every second for 3 seconds for tier 1 and is more powerful at higher tiers. On attack, this status is refreshed (cannot stack). Ignores defense. 0 Might interact with spells, abilities and passives in the future to become more powerful.
Focus_Speed: "+x" Archers only. Increases the speed that the archer can focus their shots. 1 (takes 3 seconds to fully focus)
Health_Regen: "+x/s" Changes rate of health regeneration (in health per second). Health is out of 100. 2/s (tbc - maybe 1/s?) Shouldn't normally be above 5/s, as this removes a need for the player to eat.
Looting: "+x%" Changes chance of recieving any item (e.g: unidentified item, gold, quest items) from a corpse. 50%
Poison: "+x/ys" Weapons only. Deals bonus damage to the enemy (x) over a time period (y). This ability can stack. E.g: 4/2s deals 2 damage per second for 2 seconds. 0
Reflection: "+x%" Changes the amount of damage dealt back to enemies when you are attacked, as a percentage of the damage that they deal to you. 0%
Stun: "+xs" Weapons only. Sets the amount of time that you stun an enemy for after you attack. When stunned, an enemy cannot move, attack, regen or channel. 0s
Swim_Speed: "+x/s" Changes player's movement speed in water. Measured in pixels per second (one tile = 60 pixels). 60/s
Walk_Speed: "+x/s" Changes player's movement speed on land. Measured in pixels per second (one tile = 60 pixels). 180/s
