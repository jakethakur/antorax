Archer

Archers have a low defence but can outrun their enemies and attack them from a distance.

Attack method: Ranged

Mage

Mages use an arsenal of arcane magic to heal and teleport from a moderate range.

Attack method: Aura

Knight

Knights may be melee, however they have a large range of damage and defence spells.

Attack method: Melee
